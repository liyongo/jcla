John Cowen

John Cowen has been living and working as a landscape designer in the Hamptons for the past 25+ years. Since 1994 he has operated his own design and build company.

The company has been involved in projects ranging from $500 to $2m+.

John has been involved in the landscape industry for 30+ years including 8 years of training as a Landscape Architect at Greenwich University, including a Horticultural degree equivalent from Merrist Wood College, Surrey, England.

The combined experience, professionalism and knowledge within John Cowen Landscape Associates Ltd. makes the company unique and stand out from other landscape companies in the Hamptons.
