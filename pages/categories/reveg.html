Revegetation

JCLA has followed the basic principles of landscape sustainability and used organic materials and native plant material since the company’s inception in 1994. It is important that we treat the environment locally and the planet as a whole with thoughtfulness and sustainability. We are merely the curators of this planet for the time we are here, and need to be able to pass a healthy globe onto future generations. This requires us to be ‘responsible’ for what we do to our properties and what we put into the soil and therefore the water supply and the entire ecosystem.

We have successfully assisted clients who require re-vegetation and sustainability with the planning and planting of ‘native’ plantings along with other ‘sustainable’ landscape elements. We think ‘green’ and use organic materials wherever possible.
