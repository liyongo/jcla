Estates

Estate properties are grand in scale and atmosphere, consisting of appropriately proportioned design elements drawn with broad ‘strokes’ and a certain sophisticated simplicity. The estate property is often filled with axial vantages and distinct sculptural elements, coordinated with ‘garden’ rooms of differing character. Clipped boxwood hedges may braid together in a formal knot garden or hybrid Rose gardens form a scented oasis and cutting garden. Whether formal or grand, an estate garden will give the finishing touches to your property, bringing together Architecture and Landscape.
