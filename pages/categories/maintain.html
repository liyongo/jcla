Maintenance

Property Care: Once we have designed and built your new beautiful landscape, who better to tend your landscape than the company that knows your property so well? We can create a maintenance plan that fits your needs and budget. We will attend to your landscape throughout the seasons, from spring clean-up through to winter protection, pruning and fertilizing at the appropriate times, to ensure the health and beauty of all the parts of your landscape.

Lawn Care: Part of being a design and build firm is being there at every aspect of the work. This concept has lead us to offer high-end lawn care, keeping your lawn green, lush and healthy throughout the season.
