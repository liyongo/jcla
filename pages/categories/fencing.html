Fencing

“Good fences make good neighbors” is a phrase we appreciate. After all, it is true that everyone wants privacy and to feel safe on their own property.

There are many styles of fences, differing materials, and different uses for fences: such as a small split rail fence at around 4 feet—used in a rustic setting to delineate the property line)—or a 6 feet high stockade fence to keep what's happening inside the fence–inside the fence. JCLA Ltd. has installed a large range of fences for many different purposes. It is my job as a landscape designer to help you decide which style and material is right for your property.
