Deer Resistant

Due to the large deer population in our area, creating a planting scheme, which is visually interesting without being inviting to the palates of the deer, can be a challenge. If not done properly, deer damage can be devastating to your property.

We have established a variety of trees, shrubs and perennials that are resistant to deer and still create visually appealing plant compositions. These plantings in combination with visually unobtrusive deer fencing can help to create a deer resistant property.
