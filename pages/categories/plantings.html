Planting & Gardens

Choosing the right plants for the right climates is a skill and an art form that comes with proper training, experience, and having an ‘eye’ for the right planting scheme for a particular situation.

Grouping the same plants can create a bold visual effect—as opposed to one of this plant and another of that plant, which tends to look incohesive.

We also make sure to consider the issues especially prevalent in the Hamptons; like the large Deep population and the salt-laden winds coming in from the ocean. JCLA Ltd. has the knowledge and experience to know which plants to use and not use so that your garden is safe from foragers and the sea breeze!
